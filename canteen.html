College Canteen

Healthy, hygienic, and affordable food for students

Canteen Overview

The college canteen serves as a vibrant space where students and staff can relax, refresh, and enjoy nutritious meals in a friendly environment.

The canteen follows strict hygiene standards and offers a variety of food options catering to diverse tastes.

Food & Beverages

  • Breakfast, lunch, and snacks
  • South Indian and North Indian dishes
  • Fresh juices and beverages
  • Tea, coffee, and refreshments
  • Affordable student-friendly pricing

Canteen Facilities

  • Spacious seating area
  • Clean dining environment
  • Well-ventilated space
  • Filtered drinking water
  • Quick service counters
  • Separate waste disposal
  • Hygienic food preparation
  • Regular quality checks
  • Student-friendly atmosphere

Canteen Objectives

The canteen aims to provide safe, nutritious, and affordable food while creating a comfortable social space for students.

  • To promote healthy eating habits
  • To maintain high hygiene standards
  • To offer quality food at reasonable prices
