Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science)

Transforming data into meaningful insights and intelligence

Overview

Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science) is a specialized branch that focuses on extracting knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data using scientific methods, algorithms, and systems.

This program combines computer science, statistics, mathematics, and domain knowledge to analyze large datasets and support data-driven decision-making.

Vision & Mission

Vision:

To create skilled data science professionals capable of solving complex problems using data analytics and intelligent systems.

Mission:

  • To provide strong foundations in data science and analytics.
  • To promote research and innovation in data-driven technologies.
  • To prepare students for industry, research, and higher education.

Core Subjects

  • Programming with Python & R
  • Statistics and Probability
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Machine Learning Fundamentals
  • Big Data Analytics

Advanced Learning Areas

  • Data Mining and Warehousing
  • Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Predictive Analytics
  • Business Intelligence
  • Data Visualization

Laboratories & Infrastructure

The Data Science department provides modern laboratories and high-performance computing systems to support analytics, visualization, and research activities.

  • Data Analytics & Visualization Lab
  • Machine Learning & Big Data Lab
  • Python, R, Hadoop, Spark environments
  • Cloud-based data platforms
  • High-speed internet and licensed tools

Career Opportunities

Graduates of CSE (Data Science) are highly demanded across industries that rely on data-driven insights.

  • Data Scientist
  • Data Analyst
  • Business Analyst
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Big Data Engineer
  • AI Data Specialist
  • Research Analyst
  • Data Consultant
  • Higher Studies & Research

Why Choose Data Science?

Data is the backbone of modern decision-making. From healthcare and finance to e-commerce and artificial intelligence, data science drives innovation everywhere.

Choosing CSE (Data Science) opens doors to high-paying careers, global opportunities, and the ability to shape the future through intelligent data analysis.
