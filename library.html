Central Library

A hub of knowledge, learning, and research excellence

Library Overview

The Central Library is one of the most important academic facilities of the college. It supports teaching, learning, and research activities by providing access to a wide range of information resources.

The library offers a calm and student-friendly environment that encourages reading, self-study, and intellectual growth.

Library Resources

  • Textbooks and reference books for all engineering branches
  • National and international journals
  • E-books and e-journals
  • Magazines and newspapers
  • Project reports and previous question papers

Library Facilities

  • Spacious reading halls
  • Digital library access
  • High-speed internet
  • Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC)
  • Reference and circulation section
  • Reprography services
  • Quiet study zones
  • Extended reading hours
  • Supportive library staff

Library Objectives

The primary objective of the library is to provide timely access to quality information resources and to support academic excellence.

  • To support curriculum and research needs
  • To promote reading and lifelong learning
  • To provide digital and print learning resources
