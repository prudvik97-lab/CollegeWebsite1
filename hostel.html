College Hostel Facility

Safe, comfortable, and student-friendly residential environment

Overview

The college hostel provides a comfortable and secure living environment for students coming from different regions. It is designed to support academic focus, personal growth, and community living.

Separate hostels are available for boys and girls with round-the-clock supervision and modern facilities.

Hostel Facilities

  • Spacious and well-ventilated rooms
  • 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance
  • Clean drinking water and power backup
  • Study halls and common rooms
  • High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Indoor games and recreational areas

Mess & Hygiene

The hostel mess provides nutritious and hygienic food prepared under strict quality standards. Regular cleaning and maintenance ensure a healthy living environment for all students.

Safety & Discipline

Student safety is a top priority. The hostel follows strict discipline policies with wardens available at all times to assist students.

  • 24/7 warden supervision
  • Biometric / register entry system
  • Anti-ragging policy strictly enforced

Comfortable Campus Living

The hostel life encourages teamwork, discipline, and lifelong friendships while providing a home-like atmosphere that supports academic success.
