CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)

Building intelligent systems for a smarter future

Overview

Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) is a specialized branch focused on creating intelligent systems that can learn, adapt, and make decisions with minimal human intervention.

The program blends computer science fundamentals with advanced AI and ML techniques to solve complex real-world problems in automation, data analysis, and intelligent decision-making.

Vision & Mission

Vision:

To develop competent AI & ML engineers capable of driving innovation, research, and ethical artificial intelligence solutions.

Mission:

  • To impart strong foundations in AI, ML, and computer science.
  • To encourage innovation, research, and problem-solving skills.
  • To prepare graduates for global careers and advanced research.

Laboratories & Infrastructure

Career Opportunities

Graduates of CSE (AI & ML) are in high demand across industries due to the rapid growth of intelligent technologies.

Why Choose CSE (AI & ML)?

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are transforming every industry, from healthcare and finance to robotics and smart cities.

Choosing CSE (AI & ML) empowers students to work on cutting-edge technologies and build solutions that shape the future of the world.
