No Ragging Policy

Zero tolerance towards ragging ensuring a safe and respectful campus

Our Commitment

The college strictly follows a zero-tolerance policy towards ragging. Any form of ragging—physical, mental, verbal, or psychological—is strictly prohibited on campus and in hostels.

We are committed to providing a safe, friendly, and supportive environment where every student feels secure and respected.

Anti-Ragging Measures

  • Strict enforcement of UGC anti-ragging guidelines
  • Anti-Ragging Committee and Squad
  • Awareness programs and orientations for students
  • 24/7 monitoring in hostels and campus areas
  • Immediate disciplinary action against offenders

Student Support & Reporting

Students can report any ragging-related incidents without fear. Confidentiality is maintained, and prompt action is taken to ensure justice and safety.

  • Dedicated grievance redressal system
  • Faculty mentors and counselors
  • Helpline numbers and email support

Safe Campus Culture

Our institution promotes discipline, respect, and harmony among students. A ragging-free campus helps students focus on learning, personal growth, and building a positive academic experience.
