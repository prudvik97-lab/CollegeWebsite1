Sports & Physical Education

Building strength, teamwork, and sportsmanship

Sports Facilities

The college encourages physical fitness and overall development through excellent sports and recreation facilities. Students are motivated to participate in indoor and outdoor sports activities.

  • Large outdoor playgrounds
  • Cricket, Football, Volleyball & Athletics
  • Indoor games like Chess, Carrom & Table Tennis
  • Annual sports meet and inter-college tournaments
  • Professional coaching and training support

Benefits of Sports

Sports help students develop discipline, leadership skills, teamwork, and a healthy lifestyle, contributing to their overall personality development.
